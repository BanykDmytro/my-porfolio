Jett - Valorant agent profile

Representing South Korea, Jett is fast and elusive, allowing her to take risks others can’t afford. In any skirmish, she flanks her enemies and swiftly eliminates disoriented opponents.

Jett's story

Soo Han is a girl from South Korea, possibly from the Insadong area in Seoul. She used to be a chef, but a terrible accident happened in her restaurant because of her uncontrollable powers. The restaurant was destroyed, and she ran away. Later, Soo Han joined the VALORANT Protocol organization and became an agent named Jett. During a mission in Venice, there was an explosion, and someone who looked like Jett was blamed. Although the real Jett was innocent, many people saw her as a threat. It was later revealed that the culprit was her double from another universe. All accusations against Jett were dropped, but because of the organization's secrecy, few people believed her. To ordinary people, she is still a dangerous Radiant. Unlike her evil copy, the real Jett is considered a hero among Radiants, as shown by statues and special cards.

Jett's ability

Instantly throws a projectile that turns into a brief vision-blocking cloud upon hitting a surface. HOLD the ability key to curve the smoke in the direction of your crosshair.

Jett instantly propels herself upward.

Activates a brief burst of wind. Use the ability again to make Jett dash in the direction she’s moving. If Jett is standing still, she will dash forward.

Equips a set of highly accurate throwing knives. Press the FIRE button to throw a single knife and replenish your knives upon a kill. Press the ALT. FIRE button to throw all remaining knives at once, but they won’t recharge on a kill. The ultimate requires 7 points to charge.
